Melbourne City - a cycling city

Melbourne city recognises the need for cycling investment in cycling infrastructure to improve safety and handle the increased participation in cycling. The City of Melbourne released their Bicycle Plan 2012 - 2016 which detailed the investments and initiatives selected to achieve their target of lowering the bicycle accident rates.

This project uses data from the 2012 - 2016 and 2016 - 2020 Bicycle Plans, Vic Roads Crash Statistics and independent reports and cyclist counts in order to examine whether the investments undertaken by the City of Melbourne have been effective
